  • Hydro Quebec Industrial Visit

    We will have an industrial visit to Hydro-Québec’s research institute facilities during this September, for both graduate and undergraduate ECSE students. This technical visit would allow students, in the similar area of expertise, to be exposed to IREQ's innovative technologies. Our main aims from this visit include:
    1. Educational tour of labs and facilities
    2. Technical overview about current projects
    3. Internship/Full-time opportunities (this option in particular, can be very attractive for graduate students)
    Those interested in participating in this industrial visit can contact us on ieee.mcgill@gmail.com. There are limited seats and preference will be given to McGill IEEE & WIE members.
