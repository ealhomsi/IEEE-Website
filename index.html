Who we are

We are a group of undergraduate and graduate electrical & computer engineering and computer science students who want to have fun, make friends, meet new people and learn about research and new innovations.

We offer a convivial platform for networking, volunteerism, soft skills and leadership, discounts, awards and recognition.
