About us

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is one of the largest international professional organizations dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests to a career in engineering. We are a faction of this enormous platform, motivated to enhance active participation of female engineers at McGill in various curricula in engineering and science.

Who are WiE?close

A part of the IEEE Women in Engineering, McGill IEEE WIE is an affinity group of McGill IEEE Student Branch. This group is primarily made of but not limited to the women undergraduate and graduate students of the Engineering Faculty of McGill.
