Furkan Ercan

Chairperson

Furkan Ercanclose

Major: Electrical Engineering, Ph.D.
Research Area: Error Correcting Codes / Decoder Hardware Design.
Biography: Furkan is pursuing a Ph.D. degree in ECE department of McGill University. His research focus is on design and verification of cutting edge error correcting code decoding algorithms in hardware platform for upcoming 5G communication technologies. He has been an active IEEE Student Member since 2010; when he founded the METU NCC IEEE Student Branch, at the university where he had his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees. His ambitions for McGill IEEE Student Branch includes to establish an environment where the members could improve their soft skills along with their technical knowledge.close

Mohammad Afshari

Vice-Chairperson

Mohammad Afshariclose

Major: Electrical and Computer Engineering, Ph.D.
Biography: Mohammad Afshari is a Ph.D candidate in ECE department at McGill University. He has been an active IEEE Student Member since 2012. He works under the supervision of Prof. Aditya Mahajan. His research interests are in the areas of Decentralized control, Stochastic control and Multi-agent systems. Particularly, he is working on stochastic team optimal problems and distributed estimation. As a goal, he is looking forward to empowering individuals to make a positive difference, building a society based on volunteer-staff partnership, and providing more opportunities and ideas to McGill IEEE student branch.close

Wasim Firdaus

Treasurer

Wasim Firdausclose

Major: Electrical Engineering, M.Eng.
Research Area: Photonics.
Biography: Wasim completed his undergraduate degree from Islamic University of Technology (IUT), Bangladesh in 2015 and currently he is enrolled in the Master of Engineering program in ECE department of McGill University. His research interest is in photonics field. His journey started with the simulation and characteristic analysis of plasmonic waveguides and currently his research topic is fabrication of efficient chalcogenide microwire as laser source. Since 2014, he has been a member of IUT IEEE Student branch and got selected as treasurer for the year 2015. As a member of the executive committee of McGill IEEE Student Branch, his motivation is to ensure smooth conduct of all its activities and uphold this branch as a perfect platform for students to enhance career skills. close

Jhelum Chakravortyclose

Major: Electrical and Computer Engineering, Ph.D.

Biography: Jhelum is pursuing a Ph.D. degree in ECE department of McGill University. She works under the supervision of Prof. Aditya Mahajan.
